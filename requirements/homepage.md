# Homepage Requirements - SolusIcerai.com

## Page Overview

The homepage is the primary landing page for SolusIcerai.com. It serves as an introduction to the law firm, showcasing expertise in divorce law services, building trust through testimonials, and guiding visitors toward service inquiries.

**URL**: `/` (root)
**Route**: Home
**SEO Title**: "SolusIcerai - Konsultan Hukum Perceraian Profesional Indonesia"
**Meta Description**: "Layanan hukum perceraian terpercaya di Indonesia. Konsultasi dengan ahli hukum berpengalaman untuk membantu proses perceraian Anda."

---

## Page Structure & Sections

### 1. Hero/Banner Section
**Purpose**: Make strong first impression and establish credibility

**Content Requirements**:
- Compelling headline (e.g., "Solusi Hukum Perceraian Terpercaya Indonesia")
- Supporting subheading
- Call-to-action button ("Konsultasi Sekarang" / "Book Consultation")
- Background image or gradient overlay
- Optional: Professional image of firm/team

**Design Specifications**:
- Full viewport height (100vh)
- Responsive text sizing
- Clear text hierarchy
- High contrast for readability
- Mobile-optimized (stack elements vertically)

**Content Example**:
```
Headline: "Percayakan Masalah Perceraian Anda kepada Para Ahli"
Subheading: "Konsultasi Hukum Profesional untuk Proses Perceraian yang Lancar"
CTA: "Mulai Konsultasi Gratis" → links to Contact page
```

---

### 2. About Message Section
**Purpose**: Brief introduction to the firm and its values

**Content Requirements**:
- Short about statement (2-3 paragraphs)
- Company mission/values highlight
- Key differentiators
- "Learn More" button linking to `/about` page
- Optional: Icon/image element

**Layout**:
- Text left, image right (desktop)
- Stacked (mobile)
- Maximum 80% of viewport width
- White space around content

**Content Example**:
```
Solusi Icerai adalah firma hukum terkemuka yang berspesialisasi dalam
layanan perceraian dan hukum keluarga di Indonesia. Dengan pengalaman
lebih dari 15 tahun, kami telah membantu ribuan klien melalui proses
perceraian mereka dengan profesionalisme dan kepedulian.

Kami percaya bahwa setiap klien layak mendapatkan representasi legal
yang kuat dan solusi yang disesuaikan dengan situasi unik mereka.
```

---

### 3. Our Services Preview Section
**Purpose**: Showcase main service offerings

**Content Requirements**:
- Service categories (3-5 main services)
- Each service includes:
  - Icon/visual representation
  - Service name
  - Brief description (2-3 sentences)
  - Link to Services page

**Layout**:
- Grid layout (responsive: 1 col mobile, 2-3 cols tablet, 3-4 cols desktop)
- Card-based design
- Hover effects
- Icon at top of each card

**Services to Include**:
1. Konsultasi Hukum Perceraian
2. Persiapan Dokumen Legal
3. Mediasi Perceraian
4. Representasi di Pengadilan
5. Penyelesaian Aset dan Harta Gono-Gini

### 4. Testimonials Section
**Purpose**: Build social proof and credibility

**Content Requirements**:
- 3-5 client testimonials
- Each testimonial includes:
  - Client quote (actual review, anonymized)
  - Client name or "Anonymous"
  - Profession/context
  - Star rating (1-5 stars)
  - Optional: Photo (anonymized avatar if real photo not available)

**Layout**:
- Carousel/slider (optional)
- Cards in grid layout
- Vertical scroll or horizontal swipe on mobile
- Responsive spacing

**Testimonial Example**:
```
"Tim Solusi Icerai sangat profesional dan memahami situasi kami dengan
baik. Mereka memberikan solusi yang adil dan proses yang lancar.
Sangat merekomendasikan untuk siapa pun yang membutuhkan bantuan hukum
perceraian."
- Nama Klien, Jakarta
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
```

---

### 5. Case Studies Section
**Purpose**: Demonstrate expertise through successful case examples

**Content Requirements**:
- 3-4 case study summaries
- Each case includes:
  - Case title/description (without personal details)
  - Situation overview
  - Legal approach taken
  - Outcome/result
  - Lessons learned (optional)

**Layout**:
- Timeline or sequential card layout
- Desktop: 2 columns
- Mobile: 1 column stacked
- Visual separation between cases

**Case Study Example**:
```
Case Title: "Penyelesaian Harta Gono-Gini Kompleks"

Situasi:
Klien dengan pernikahan 20+ tahun memiliki aset bisnis dan properti
yang kompleks yang perlu dibagi secara adil.

Pendekatan Hukum:
- Analisis mendalam terhadap nilai aset
- Negosiasi langsung dengan pihak lain
- Mediasi oleh pengadilan

Hasil:
- Penyelesaian adil dalam 6 bulan
- Klien menerima 45% dari total aset
- Proses tanpa konflik emosional yang berkepanjangan
```

- Primary keyword: "Hukum perceraian Indonesia"

## Content Tone & Style

- **Professional**: Conveys expertise and credibility
- **Compassionate**: Shows understanding of sensitive situation
- **Clear**: Simple, jargon-free language (explain legal terms)
- **Trustworthy**: Honest and transparent
- **Action-oriented**: Clear next steps for visitors

---

## User Journey on Homepage

1. User lands on page (Hero grabs attention)
2. User learns about firm (About section)
3. User explores services (Services preview)
4. User gains confidence (Testimonials)
5. User sees proven results (Case studies)
6. User reads interesting content (Article previews)
7. User takes action (Contact CTA)
