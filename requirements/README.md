# Website Requirements - SolusIcerai.com

## Project Context

**Website Name**: SolusIcerai.com
**Organization**: Solusi Icerai (Legal Law Firm)
**Specialization**: Hukum Perceraian (Divorce Law) in Indonesia
**Primary Focus**: Comprehensive legal services related to divorce matters in Indonesia

## Mission & Vision

**Mission**: Provide accessible, professional, and compassionate legal guidance for individuals navigating divorce proceedings in Indonesia.

**Vision**: Become the trusted legal partner for divorce law matters in Indonesia, known for expertise, reliability, and client-focused service.

## Target Audience

- Indonesian individuals seeking divorce legal services
- People considering legal separation
- Individuals currently in divorce proceedings
- Families requiring legal guidance on family matters

## Website Purpose

1. **Inform**: Educate visitors about divorce law services and expertise
2. **Build Trust**: Establish credibility through testimonials and case studies
3. **Convert**: Guide visitors to contact and book services
4. **Engage**: Provide valuable content through articles and resources

## Key Features

### 1. Homepage
Primary landing page featuring:
- Professional hero banner
- Brief about message
- Service overview
- Testimonials/social proof
- Case studies showcase
- Contact call-to-action

### 2. About Us
Detailed company information:
- Expanded about message
- Company mission and values
- Team expertise (if applicable)
- Why choose us section

### 3. Services
Comprehensive service catalog:
- Divorce law services
- Service details and processes
- Benefits and outcomes
- Call to action

### 4. Contact
Service booking interface:
- Contact form
- Call to action for service booking
- Expected response time
- Contact information

### 5. Articles/Blog
Educational content section:
- Article listings
- Article detail pages
- Thought leadership content
- SEO-optimized content

### 6. Global Elements
Consistent design across all pages:
- Header with navigation
- Footer with links and information
- Color scheme and typography
- Responsive design system

## Technical Requirements

### Frontend Framework
- **React** with TypeScript for type safety
- Functional components with hooks
- Component-based architecture

### Styling
- **Tailwind CSS v4** for all styling
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Consistent design system
- Dark mode consideration (optional)

### Content Management
- Static content (no CMS requirement initially)
- Contact form handling
- Article management

### Performance Requirements
- Page load time < 3 seconds
- Mobile responsive (all screen sizes)
- SEO-optimized structure
- Accessibility compliant (WCAG 2.1 AA minimum)

### Browser Support
- Chrome (latest)
- Firefox (latest)
- Safari (latest)
- Edge (latest)
- Mobile browsers

## Design Principles

1. **Professional**: Legal services require trust and professionalism
2. **Accessible**: Content must be easily navigable and understandable
3. **Responsive**: Must work perfectly on all devices
4. **Fast**: Quick load times are essential
5. **Clear**: Information hierarchy is important
6. **Trustworthy**: Design conveys expertise and reliability

## Content Language

- **Primary**: Bahasa Indonesia (Indonesian)
- **Secondary**: English (optional for international visitors)

## SEO Considerations

- Keyword optimization for divorce law services
- Meta tags and descriptions
- Sitemap generation
- Mobile optimization
- Page speed optimization
- Structured data markup

## Analytics & Tracking

- Track form submissions
- Monitor page visits
- User behavior analysis
- Conversion tracking (service inquiries)

## Security & Privacy

- SSL/HTTPS encryption
- Privacy policy implementation
- GDPR/local data privacy compliance
- Secure contact form handling
- Client confidentiality

## Future Enhancements (Out of Scope)

- Blog/CMS integration
- Client portal

- Multi-language support
- Mobile app

## Success Metrics

- Website fully responsive and functional
- Professional appearance and brand alignment
- Clear navigation and user experience
- High accessibility score (>90)
- Fast page load times (<3 seconds)
- Positive user feedback and inquiries
